About Me

I am a writer and historian, currently teaching in Harvard's History and Literature program. My main portfolio page (very much a work in progress!) has links to my writing and my other programming projects.

Why are you taking this course?

I'm interested in digital media and am taking this course to further my web skills. I'd like to be able to work as a freelance back-end web developer in the future.

What previous experience do you have with programming?

I have a background in programming in Java. I took CSE10b (Introduction to Computer Science Using Java II) last semester and am currently enrolled in CSE22 (Data Structures). This summer I took a digital media course (Explorations in Screen-Based and Physical Computing) and learned a lot more JavaScript. I've picked up HTML and CSS through a variety of sources, mostly online and some short in-person classes at WCC and GDI in Boston. And I attended RailsBridge last spring, though it was a bit beyond me at that point. I've also studied digital mapping using ArcGIS.
